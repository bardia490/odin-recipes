Description

This recipe makes chicken- and cheese-filled corn tortillas baked under a creamy sauce infused with hot peppers. Adjust the amount of chopped chiles to suit your taste!

Additional information

  • Prep Time:
    • 30 mins
  • Cook Time:
    • 30 mins
  • Additional Time:
    • 15 mins
  • Total Time:
    • 1 hrs
  • Servings:
    • 6

Ingredients You'll Need for chicken enchiladas

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or more as needed
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 3 cooked boneless skinless chicken breast halves, shredded
  • 12 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided
  • ¾ cup minced onion
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chiles, drained
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry tortillas (one at a time) for 5 seconds on each side to soften and make them pliable. Add more oil to the pan as needed.
  4. Step 4: Drain between layers of paper towel and keep warm.
  5. Step 5: Divide chicken, 10 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, and onion among 12 tortillas.
  6. Step 6: Roll up each tortilla and place seam-side down in the prepared pan.
  7. Step 7: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and whisk until mixture begins to boil. Slowly add chicken broth, stirring with a whisk until thickened. Mix in sour cream and green chiles, stirring occasionally. Heat thoroughly but do not boil.
  8. Step 8: Pour mixture over enchiladas.
  9. Step 9: Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and heated through, about 20 minutes. Top with remaining Monterey Jack cheese and bake for 5 more minutes.
  10. Step 10: Garnish with chopped green onions and cilantro.
